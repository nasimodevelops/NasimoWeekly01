Behind the Production

The JazzHop Dream really means a lot to me in this point of my journey as an Independent Artist.

This semester in college, I enrolled in a songwriting and comopsition class. Without much thought about what I was getting myself into, the resulting knowledge and experience I gained throughout the semester were massive.

Click Here!

Weekly Progression

Right off the bat, I wanted to prove to my professor how much making music really means to me. I made at least one production a week throughout the entire semester to present to him, to get his feedback and see how I can improve for the next track.

My Endless Learning Journey

Each monday, my one-on-one lesson with my professor was focused on learning music theory. Learning the mechanics behind the music to expand my horizons as a songwriter.

The JazzHop Dream Come True

All those weeks practicing my music theory and piano skills, I began to grow a large appreciation for Jazz. At the same time however, I was also listening to a ton of old school hip hop like Outcast and Nas. I combined both of those genres and began producing beats that incorporated a bit of both worlds of sound.
